# Product Steering — Luma

Luma is a cozy customizable finance space.

Core identity:
- aesthetic-first
- personal dashboard
- custom background/theme/character
- simple finance tracking
- soft budgeting
- saving goals
- useful reports
- optional AI shortcut

Luma must not feel like:
- corporate fintech
- spreadsheet finance
- hardcore productivity
- aggressive budgeting
- gamified challenge app

Primary UX rule:
Manual transaction input is the default. AI is secondary.

Navigation:
Bottom nav must be:
Home | Transaksi | Target | Laporan

Settings:
Theme, character, and background customization live in Settings.

Budget:
Budget is accessed from Home → Budget Detail.

Tone:
Casual Indonesian, soft, supportive, non-judgmental.
